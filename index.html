Plant Guardian

Smart Plant Monitoring Robot

Using AI and IR sensors to detect plant health

Project Overview

This robot autonomously moves in a defined pattern, capturing plant photos every 1.5 hours. AI processes images to detect signs of disease or water deficiency and sends notifications to the user. Currently we can maintain 85% accuracy in plant health detection. We hope to improve this up to 97% accuracy with more data and user feedback.
