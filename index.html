Plant Guardian

Smart Plant Monitoring Robot

Using AI and IR sensors to detect plant health

Project Overview

This robot autonomously moves in a defined pattern, capturing plant photos every 1.5 hours. AI processes images to detect signs of disease or water deficiency and sends notifications to the user. Currently we can maintain 85% accuracy in plant health detection. We hope to improve this up to 97% accuracy with more data and user feedback.

Key Features

  • 📷 **Automated Plant Image Capturing**
  • 🧠 **AI-based Health Detection**
  • 🛠 **IR Sensors for Room Navigation**
  • 📡 **Real-time Alerts to User**

How It Works

The robot follows a **pre-defined movement pattern**, analyzing plant health through AI-driven image processing. Alerts are triggered when any **watering or disease intervention** is needed.

🌱 Benefits of AI-Based Plant Monitoring Robot

✅ **Automated Plant Health Monitoring**

With AI-driven analysis, this robot continuously checks plants for signs of disease, water deficiency, and environmental stress, ensuring timely intervention.

📸 **Consistent Image-Based Detection**

Captures high-quality plant images every **1.5 hours**, allowing AI to detect subtle changes in leaf health and growth, minimizing human effort.

🌾 **Advancing Nepal’s Agriculture**

In Nepal’s agricultural sector, this system helps farmers **detect early crop infections** and water stress, reducing losses and improving yield.

🏡 **Ideal for Home Plant Care**

Perfect for home gardens! It alerts users when plants need water or show signs of disease, ensuring healthier indoor and outdoor plants.

💧 **Smart Water Usage**

Prevents overwatering or under-watering by detecting moisture needs, promoting **sustainable water management**, crucial for Nepal’s agriculture.

⚡ **Reduces Manual Labor**

Farmers and homeowners no longer need daily inspections—this AI robot takes care of monitoring, making plant care effortless!
